The second annual Rotting Orchards Haunted House will be open on Halloween Night and will be taking donations for the Raising a Reader program. The haunted house is absolutely free and promises to be fun for all ages. This year we are adding a scare meter for regulating the scare level inside. This means you can bring your little ones for a gentle walk-through tour or get your pants scared off as an adult! The choice is yours and we can and will accommodate everyone of all ages.

Bring the kids and enjoy our labor of love for a good cause.
